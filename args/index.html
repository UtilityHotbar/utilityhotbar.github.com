List of Tools, Software, and Techniques

By beeptest

* indicates paid tool.

Website Tools

  • Obfuscator.io: Obfuscates Javascript code (makes it harder to deconstruct puzzles/cheat)
  • Dither it: Makes images dithered
  • Photomosh: Makes glitchy images/gifs
  • CyberChef: Swiss army knife for data formatting/encryption (base64, binary, simple ciphers...)
  • Google Firebase*: Web hosting and cloud/database management with free tier
  • Github Pages: Free web hosting
  • Protonmail: Free secure email accounts (Only 250 MB storage unless you do a bunch of stuff that gives you 1GB but secure and less hassle to set up than google)

Software Tools

  • Affinity Suite*: Alternative to Photoshop/InDesign/Illustrator, one time purchase with ~70% of the features
  • Wilbur: Terrain erosion simulation (mapmaking, cumbersome but powerful)
  • Audacity: Free audio editing (cumbersome but powerful)
  • Kdenlive: Free video editing (cumbersome but powerful)
  • GIMP: Free image editing (cumbersome but powerful)
  • NTSQT: VHS filter with many options (untested by me)

Command Line Tools

  • CPDF*: PDF manipulation (encryption/merging/metadata removing etc.), paid but with non-commercial license.
  • Ghostscript: PDF merging/manipulation, free and open source

Puzzle Design

  • Enigmatics: Expert puzzle designers and tools
  • Alternate Reality Game puzzle design Portal 2 developer Adam Foster walks you through designing a satisfying puzzle for the Portal 2 launch.
  • Creating the Alien Writing in Infinifactory - Zachtronics developer Zach Barth walks you through designing a tiny ARG and some examples of alien writing.

Resources/fonts

  • Indestructible Type - Excellent free and open license fonts.
  • Google Fonts - A huge amount of free and open license fonts.
  • Old Book Illustrations - Scans of old books, ready to use.

Videos

  • Making an ARG: Walker Creek - Alex Hera walks you through making an ARG.
  • Trailhead: Unfiction Research Results - Nick Nocturne walks you through ARG creation statistics and experiences from other creators.
